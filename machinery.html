We produce or supply Goods, & Services, Roll Forming Machine

Roll Forming Machine are vital tools in the world of metal shaping and fabrication. These machines are designed to gradually shape a continuous metal sheet into the desired profile. By feeding the metal through a series of rollers, Roll Forming Machine ensure consistent and precise shaping, making them invaluable in various industries.

Roll forming is a continuous bending process where a strip of metal is passed through a series of carefully designed rolls or rollers. Each set of rolls progressively shapes the metal into the desired profile, with the final product emerging as a precisely formed, uniform section. This method ensures consistent results with minimal material wastage.

We produce or supply Goods, & Services, C & Z Purlins Machine

A C & Z Purlin Roll Forming Machine is an essential apparatus utilized in the fabrication process of steel purlins, which serve as horizontal structural elements in roofing and wall construction, providing support for roof decks or cladding. Designed to produce C-shaped and Z-shaped purlins from coiled steel strip or sheet metal, this machine comprises several key components. The process begins with an uncoiler, where the steel coil is loaded, followed by a leveling and feeding device ensuring the strip's flatness and uniformity.

The steel strip then passes through the roll forming system, the central mechanism of the machine, where it is gradually shaped into the desired profile by a series of rollers. Upon reaching the desired length, a cutting device, either a flying saw or hydraulic shear, trims the purlin accordingly. Some machines may also include a punching and hole-making unit to create fastening points along the length of the purlin.

We produce or supply Goods, & Services, Cut to Length Machine

A Cut to Length Machine is a crucial piece of equipment used in various industries, particularly in metal processing and fabrication. Its primary function is to precisely cut continuous metal coils or sheets into specific lengths or sizes according to predetermined requirements. The machine typically consists of several key components, including an uncoiler for feeding the metal coils, a straightening unit to ensure the material is flat and uniform, and a cutting mechanism such as shear blades or rotary knives.

We produce or supply Goods, & Services, Crimping Curve Machine

A Crimping Curve Machine is a specialized piece of equipment used primarily in the roofing industry for shaping and crimping metal roofing panels. This machine is designed to create curved panels that are used for architectural purposes or to fit specific roof designs. The machine typically consists of a series of rollers and forming dies that work together to bend and shape the metal roofing material into the desired curve or profile. The process begins with feeding the metal panels into the machine, where they pass through a series of rollers that gradually apply pressure to bend the material to the desired curvature.

The forming dies then crimp the edges of the panel to ensure a secure fit and create a finished appearance. Crimping Curve Machines are often used in conjunction with other roofing equipment, such as roll formers and cut to length machines, to produce complete roofing systems tailored to the specifications of a particular project. They offer precision and efficiency in creating curved metal panels, making them an essential tool for roofing contractors and manufacturers involved in architectural and custom roofing projects.

We produce or supply Goods, & Services, PUF Panel Machine

A PUF (Polyurethane Foam) Panel Machine is a specialized manufacturing equipment used in the production of insulated panels commonly known as PUF panels. These panels are widely used in the construction industry for their excellent thermal insulation properties, making them ideal for applications in cold storage facilities, warehouses, refrigerated trucks, and building envelopes. The PUF panel machine consists of several key components, including a double belt conveyor system for feeding the metal sheets, a high-pressure foaming machine, and a double belt laminator. The process begins with the metal sheets being fed into the machine, where they pass through the foaming machine where polyurethane foam is injected between the metal sheets to form a sandwich-like structure.

The panels then enter the laminator, where they are pressed together to ensure proper bonding and uniform thickness. The panels are then cut to the desired length using a cutting device, and the finished panels are stacked for further processing or transportation. PUF panel machines are highly automated and computer-controlled, ensuring precise control over the foaming and laminating processes, resulting in high-quality insulated panels with consistent thermal performance.

We produce or supply Goods, & Services, Sliting Roll Forming Machine

A Slitting Roll Forming Machine is a specialized piece of equipment used in metal fabrication and processing industries for cutting and shaping metal coils into narrower strips or sheets with precise dimensions. This machine is particularly useful for transforming wide coils of metal into narrower strips, which are commonly used in various applications such as roofing, cladding, and automotive components.

The metal coil passes through the slitting knives, which make longitudinal cuts to create narrower strips of the desired width. The machine can be adjusted to produce strips of different widths by changing the settings of the slitting knives. Additionally, some slitting roll forming machines may include other features such as edge trimming, recoiling, or stacking capabilities to further customize the finished strips.

These machines are highly versatile and can process various types of metal coils, including steel, aluminum, and copper, making them essential tools in metal processing facilities for producing precision-cut metal strips tailored to specific requirements.

We produce or supply Goods, & Services, Hydraulic De-Coilers

Hydraulic De-Coilers are essential machines used in metal processing and fabrication industries, particularly in the production of coils, sheets, and strips from flat metal stock. These machines are designed to efficiently and effectively unwind metal coils or reels of various sizes and weights, allowing for smooth and continuous feeding of the material into subsequent processing equipment such as roll forming machines, press brakes, or shearing machines.

The hydraulic de-coiler typically consists of a sturdy frame with hydraulic cylinders or motors that exert force to unwind the metal coil. The coil is securely mounted on a mandrel or shaft that rotates to release the metal material.

These machines are available in a range of capacities to accommodate different coil sizes and weights, and they often come equipped with safety features such as braking systems and overload protection to prevent accidents and ensure operator safety.

We produce or supply Goods, & Services, Press Break Machine

A Press Brake Machine, commonly referred to as a press brake, is a pivotal tool in metalworking and fabrication, essential for bending and shaping sheet metal and plate material into various forms with precision and accuracy. The machine operates by exerting force on the workpiece between a punch and a die, thereby forming bends, angles, or curves according to specific design requirements. Its key components typically include a hydraulic or mechanical press for generating bending force.

A flat bed or table upon which the workpiece rests during bending, and a movable ram or upper beam that holds the punch responsible for shaping the metal.Modern press brake machines often feature advanced control systems.

Modern press brake machines often feature advanced control systems, such as computer numerical control (CNC), enabling precise adjustment and management of bending parameters such as angle, length, and force.
